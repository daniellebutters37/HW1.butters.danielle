Information About Me

Here is a bit about me...

My Name is Danielle. I grew up in London, Ontario and spent most of my childhood outdoors and at the Ice Rink. Having two older brothers, all I wanted to do was hangout with them. This came with stipulations in the form of pillows duck-taped to my limbs, while facing slap-shots from 3 feet out. From there my love for hockey grew. I started playing competitive boys hockey, and in time moved to girls hockey. Eventually hockey led me to University in Minnesota. During my time in Minnesota I earned a Bachelor degree in Biochemistry. Shortly After, I took two years away from school and moved to Australia and travelled Asia. I eventually moved to Sweden and started my Master in Environmental Research dealing with water pollutants at the University of Stockholm. I dropped out after the first semester and decided that I was not enjoying my academic pursuits, and needed a change. Shortly after moving back to London, I decide to take the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe.

When I am not busy with school, I spend time with family and friends. I also enjoy my alone time, and will typically spend it reading, listening to music, biking the trails along the Thames River, and going to yoga classes.
